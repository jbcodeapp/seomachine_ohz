## Internal Linking Best Practices

When adding internal links to blog content:

1. **Link Naturally**: Only link when genuinely relevant and helpful to the reader
2. **Vary Anchor Text**: Use different phrases for the same destination URL
3. **3-5 Links Per Post**: Aim for 3-5 strategic internal links in each blog post
4. **Deep Linking**: Link to specific service, industry, or blog pages—not just the homepage
5. **Early Links Matter**: Links in the first few paragraphs carry more SEO weight
6. **Service Page Priority**: Always link to at least one relevant service page (Remote Guarding, Event-Based Security, or Virtual Doorman)
7. **Industry Pages**: Link to the relevant industry page when writing about a specific vertical
8. **Update This Map**: Add new blog posts as they are published
